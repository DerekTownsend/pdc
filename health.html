Health Center

In partnership with Lawndale Christian Health, PDC intends to build a state-of-the art Health and Fitness Center in the West Garfield Park neighborhood. We estimate that the center would provide healthcare to an estimated 6000 patients per year. The facility would also house fitness equipment, a healthy food oriented restaurant, dental care facilities and space for community program. In addition to the services the center would provide, hiring both during construction and for the 40 jobs created by the Health Center will look to the community first.
